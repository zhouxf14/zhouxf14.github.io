Introduction
In a sketching class aiming to teach students visualization information, I observed that there is common expert thinking procedures of analyzing, extracting, modeling and synthesizing information. However, this is also an essential problem that novice students have for thier information visualization work. Such observation motivated me to design an online course for learners to develop the ability to model and visualize information into different diagrams.
The learning objective of the online course is: Based on a communication purpose, how to visualize information by conceptual models/diagrams?
