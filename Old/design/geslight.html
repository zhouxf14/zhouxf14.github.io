GesLight  - Free to control your light
What is it?

This is a light control system based on natural gesture recognition. You can use your gestures to control the lighting switch and brightness in a most natural way. We use our 3D model in Unity and the technology of VR to help us present the prototype of our lighting system.



I am the user researcher for this project. I designed and conducted 35 user studies to collect corresponding gesture datasets. After analyzing the results, I built user scenarios, designed system interaction rules based on the classified experiment results and conducted summative usability testing with the prototype of the interaction system.

How does it work?

  • User Scenarios

    We defined five user scenarios, including (1) switching off the light of the current room, (2) switching on the light of the current room, (3) adjusting the brightness of the light in the current room, (4) switch off the light after leaving the room, and (5) switching on the light before entering the room. The video demo below will show you how we defined the interacting rules for each scenario.

    And according to the results of our user research, we design control gestures for corresponding user scenarios.
