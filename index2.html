About Me

My research interest and design passion lie in creating innovative, interactive systems to help people develop complex and softer forms of human intelligence, and ultimately realize their own life values.

My undergraduate major in Human Factors gave me a good understanding of how people interact with systems. Currently, the METALS (Master of Educational Technology and Applied Learning Science) program is offering me a window into academic research in the intersection of learning and technology.
