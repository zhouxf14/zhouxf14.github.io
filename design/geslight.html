GesLight  - Free to control your light
What is it?

This is a light control system based on natural gesture recognition. You can use your gestures to control the lighting switch and brightness in a most natural way. We use our 3D model in Unity and the technology of VR to help us present the prototype of our lighting system.



I am the user researcher for this project. I designed and conducted 35 user studies to collect corresponding gesture datasets. After analyzing the results, I built user scenarios, designed system interaction rules based on the classified experiment results and conducted summative usability testing with the prototype of the interaction system.

What problems does it solve?

For thousands of years, physical instruments have been used to play music. But physical instruments are usually either large in size or inconvenient to be carried with. In addition, it’s particularly difficult for university students to have a regular musical activity because of the limited space of their dorm and because of the noise. Therefore, we propose a new method of playing music, called “BoMix”. In this new input system, the user only need his or her body to make music.

How does it work?

  • User Scenarios

    We defined five user scenarios, including (1) switching off the light of the current room, (2) switching on the light of the current room, (3) adjusting the brightness of the light in the current room, (4) switch off the light after leaving the room, (5) swithing on the light before entering the room.
