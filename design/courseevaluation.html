Ping(评什么)  - A Course Online Evaluation System
What is it?

Ping(评什么) is the very first course online evaluation platform initiated by several undergraduates from Tsinghua University. On the platform, students can provide or search for the feedback for the courses they have taken or plan to take. Also, the study experience for a specific course can be shared and discussed on it.

I am the UI designer in our group. I worked hard to think about how to make it easier for students to reach the information they really want and how to make it less trouble for them to provide their own feedback for courses they have taken. I hope to provide better user experience for students to make them more willing to share their valuable information on this sharing platform.

What problems does it solve?

At the end of each semester in Tsinghua University, it is the time for students to select courses they want to take for next term. In order to decide whether to take a course or not, students need to spend lots of energy looking for extra information of some truly important aspects of a course, including the curriculum quality, the curriculum task quantity, the professor's teaching style and so on. As a result, students always spend a lot of time unnecessary seeking such information from other schoolmates who have already taken those courses. In turn, students may also have to talk about his or her experience in one course for several times, which is quite an inefficient process. In addition, it it not easy to find someone who happens to have taken the courses, which means that it is quite an uneffective process. That's how we came up with the idea to establish a course online evaluation platform to provide an opportunity for students to share more comprehensive and effective information of all the courses to our schoolmates.

How does it work?

  • Log in with student ID and search for the courses you are interested in.


  • Basic information for a course and the feedback from students who have taken the course.


  • Score a course you have taken and leave comments for it.
