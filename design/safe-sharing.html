Safe-Sharing  - A personal safe with 3 user scenarios
What is it?

This is a safe which can be shared with guests. Facial recognition and speech recognition are used as its doublet code. Once discovering unidentified users, a spare empty space will be activated and the user can only open that empty space, which will protect valuable items in the safe.

I am the independent designer and developer for this project. I designed the spatial model for product construction with SketchUp. The safe was developed with Python and Arduino programming on Galileo Board and ZOC Terminal.

What problems does it solve?

Safe is always very secret and personal space for people. But differentiate the space for it will provide opportunity for sharing the safe between the owner and guests, even "sharing" with those with bad intentions.
