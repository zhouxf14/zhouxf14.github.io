Conquer the Fatigue  - Take a rest before too late!
What is it?

This is an app help to predict your terrible physical state with the assistance of a smart bracelet to detect several physical indexes of yours. Then it can remind you to take a rest in time before your terrible physical state actually arrives and strikes you down, which really impacts your working efficiency and physical health.

I am the prototype and UI designer in our team. You can watch the video demo I made and UI design below to better understand what problem we actually want to solve with Conquer the Fatigue.
