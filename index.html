Hi, welcome!

I‘m Xiaofei, studying Industrial Engineering at Tsinghua University.

I’m passionate to create better interactions between people and technology in our daily life. Conducting research and designing for real experience built up my skillset and deeper understanding of this ever-changing, fascinating discipline, Human Computer Interaction. You could find my projects here. Also feel free to scroll down for some of the highlighted ones. Out of class and research projects, I love painting and mobile photography very much. Sometimes I draw little pieces of sketches describing my life just for fun. Music and singing, you ask? Of course!

Enjoy your visit on my site, and feel free to email me at zhouxf14@163.com

Bomixclose

Bomix is a new way for users to play music, not with instruments, but with their body. The core idea for this new input system is to control the height of a note by the position of your hand and control the rythm of music by it's shaking frequency. What's more, you can play different instruments with different parts of your body.
